## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Risk Signal Processing
The execution engine SHALL subscribe to and process exit signals generated by the risk management module. Upon receiving a risk exit signal, it MUST immediately initiate the appropriate order to close the affected position.

#### Scenario: Processing Stop-Loss Signal
- **WHEN** the execution engine receives a stop-loss exit signal for 100 units of asset A
- **THEN** it SHALL validate the position and submit a MARKET SELL order for 100 units of asset A to close the position
